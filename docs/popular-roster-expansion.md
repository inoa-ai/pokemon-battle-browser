# Popular Roster Expansion

Access date: 2026-07-07

## Selection Basis

The 20 added Pokemon were selected from the official Pokemon of the Year 2020 results, prioritizing high-ranking Pokemon that were not already in the game.

Primary source:

- Pokemon of the Year 2020 results: https://pokemon2020.pokemon.com/en-us/

The existing roster already included several top-ranked Pokemon such as Pikachu, Charizard, Gengar, Lucario, Eevee, Umbreon, Sylveon, Dragonite, Arcanine, Lapras, Mewtwo, and Absol. The expansion therefore focused on the next popular candidates that were missing.

## Implementation Notes

- Roster size increased from 24 to 44 in this expansion. The current roster is 45 after the later Jirachi addition.
- New local artwork files were added under `public/assets/pokemon-art` and later converted to transparent PNG.
- Rock type support was added for Tyranitar and Lycanroc.
- Each added Pokemon has four default moves and also participates in the move loadout candidate pool.
